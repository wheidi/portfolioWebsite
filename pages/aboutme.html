🧑🏾‍🤝‍🧑🏼 Psychology:
Understanding others and myself

"Positive emotions encourage approach-behaviour". Psychology can tell us so much about how emotions and styles of motivation can influence our behaviours. Incorporating these principles into my design process and work habits provide me a greater insight into how I can better design and work as well.

🧢 Empathy:
Wearing different hats

A big part of understanding an audience is empathizing with them. This includes any identity or community involved. Whenever I encountered ambiguity, I found that a solid starting point would almost always be to put on my user hat, become the user, and go from there.

🎇 Awe:
A sense of wonder

Often, a good, smooth experience always gave me some sense of awe. Awe itself is also affiliated with a slew of positive effects that I won't get into, but that we can definitely chat about 😉. Even something as simple as a loading animation can elicit awe, and thus is an experience that I personally aspire to craft.
